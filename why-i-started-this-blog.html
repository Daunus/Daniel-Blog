Why This Blog?

Nov 24, 2015  |  0 Min Read   |  

At this point BIP 101 was released around 4 months ago and the second ‘Scaling Bitcoin Conference’ is 2 weeks away.

There is an active debate about the direction of Bitcoin and I have many new thoughts about the economic and political issues, that I want to contribute to the debate. I want maximum exposure for these view, hence my starting this blog.

I will also be writing technical posts related to digital currencies. I will not be adding anything new here as there are many people with vastly greater knowledge.

My aim with technical posts is to improve my understanding, and the guides might prove useful to other people with similar educational aspirations.
